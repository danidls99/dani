Terra –
Terra is an imagined compost bin targeted towards city dwellers. Composting in New York City can be a hassle if your building doesn’t participate in the curbside program and you are forced to lug your organic waste across the city to a drop-off center. Terra is a compost bin designed to combat specific urban challenges including limited space, odor-control, portability, and style. Terra is targeted towards design savvy people who are also environmentally conscious. Applications of the brand system include: website, subway poster ads, product box, reusable vinyl grocery bag, and Instagram ad.
