The Power of Non-Conformity Brochure –
During my internship at the Rubin Musueum of Art, I had the responsibility to create a brochure for the Fall Series "The Power of Non-Conformity." I familiarized myself with the Rubin's brand identity guidelines while creating this piece of ephemera. This brochure was printed at GHP Media for distribution to the museum's members.
