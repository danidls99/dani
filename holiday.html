SPE Holiday Party –
Every year during my time as a student assistant at the executive dean’s office of The School of Public Engagement (SPE), I am tasked with designing the annual holiday party invitation distributed digitally to all faculty and staff at SPE. For this year’s holiday party I also created a visual identity for the venue, applied in the decorations. Additionally, I made a handmade banner displayed at the entrance of the event.
