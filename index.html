Dani Delossantos is a graphic designer and artist based in New York. She is currently a junior in the Communication Design Program at Parsons School of Design. Along with her major in CD, Dani is also minoring in Art and Design History. She has experience in typography, editorial design, web design and interaction design. Dani strongly coincides her design work with her morals and strong work ethic. She loves art, astrology, food, music, and spending time with family and friends.
