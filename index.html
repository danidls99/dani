Dani Delossantos is a New York City based designer. She currently attends Parsons School of Design where she is majoring in Communication Design and minoring in Art and Design History. She specializes in print and type design in the context of brand identities. Dani has interned at the Rubin Museum of Art and currently works as a freelance designer focusing on advertising and event marketing. She loves food culture, 70s dad bands, and beekeeping.
