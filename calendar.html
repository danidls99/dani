Rubin Fall 2019 Calendar –
For each season at the Rubin Museum of Art, a calendar brochure of museum events is mailed to all the museum’s members and available in the museum itself. During my internship, I was tasked to design the calendar for the Fall 2019 season while sticking to the museum’s brand identity and yearly theme. I was also invited to overlook its printing at GHP Media in Connecticut. See here for a link to the virtual booklet.
