Queenly –
For the final project of the class called Honeybee Colonies: Art, Design, Science and Culture, I created a honeybee sheet mask and brand system. Queenly is a brand system based on aesthetics and values surrounding the Art Nouveau movement/“le femme nouvelle” and the magic and regality of the feminine figures of Ancient Egyptian queens. The values behind the brand’s visual identity also supports the idea of the queen bee of a hive and the royal jelly fed to larvae queens. Along with the creation of the brand, I physically created the mask formula utilizing bee products such as honey, royal jelly, and propolis.
Applications of the brand include package design, subway poster ad, Instagram profile, and website mockup.
